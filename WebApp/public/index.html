But first we would like to tell you our story.
This Application is a project for a course at our university. It is supposed to support sustainability in a way that we want companies to make their products last longer. Some do speak of planned obsolescence. The competition is big but the huge companies still can do what they want because the consumers will never know if their devices broke because they were old or if they broke because the company planned it in advance. There is nothing we can do to get the answer except for sharing our experiences with everyone and support the companies that really care about us and our environment.

The idea was that you can save all of your devices to have an overview of the devices you have. The App saves the brand, model, price, date of purchase, condition in which the device was bought, and additionally it saves a picture of the receipt so you do not have to go through everything you own just to get the receipt and check if the garanty is still valid. If a device broke you can make a fault report so the other users can see what part of your device broke and the reason for that. You can also add if the device was repaired and if the garanty took care of it. Your data will be saved in an anonimous way so no one can see that you actually made the fault report, even we can not see that you made it. The Fault reports help us to create statistics of the broken devices, to see if there actually are devices that break after a specific time at a specific part. Of course you also benefit from this because the next time you buy a new laptop for example you can see if there are already some users that have bad experiences with a spesific model and you will always know where you have your receipts! We hope that you will have fun with the app and spread the intention of it because when everyone helps the companies may start caring about us and the environment and not only their money.
